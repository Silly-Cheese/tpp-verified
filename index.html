What This Verifies

This portal verifies volunteer confirmation documents issued by The Prayer Project, including the volunteer name, service dates, total verified hours, issue date, and current document status.

A document is only valid when the Document ID appears in this system as active and matches the information printed on the volunteer confirmation form.
